Welcome To Simon Scott Guitars

Welcome to Simon Scott Guitars and thank you for visiting my website.

Simon Scott Guitars is a business ran by luthier, musician and guitar teacher Simon Scott.

Simon Scott Guitars offers a wide range of services, to musicians of all ages and ability that includes custom made guitars, guitar lessons and more.

Services Include

  • Private tuition in guitar, bass and theory.

  • Online lessons and resources that will aid you in your playing and understanding.

  • Setups, repairs and modification service

  • Bespoke handcrafted guitars made from the highest quality materials and hardware. We accept custom design commissions.
    Tell us what you want and we can make It for you.

  • Shop for guitars, hardware, electronics, books, equipment, accessories, maintenance and more...
